A picture at the end of a long day;
A day of exploring and wathcing and listening.
And eating.
Hidden behind the frozen smiles and tired eyes,
Too full bellies and aching feet.
And hearts swollen with joy.
Emotions and memories to be gleened by the beholder,
But perhaps lost or misunderstood.
Like the language of a poem or notes of a song,
Meant to capture a moment in time.
Nothing more.
