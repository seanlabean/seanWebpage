Computer Science and Me

Computer science has taken an interesting, meandering path through the latter half of my life. I hated computers for a very long time. A computer had always represented a useful and required tool but one built on a razors edge from which the slightest deviation resulted in obscure errors and catastrophic pitfalls. This precarious state of being truly prevented me from engaging with computers in any meaningful way. Thoughout college I waited idly as my collegues built software, tinkered with their machines, learned. I hesitated. For years. I will hesitate no more. I can teach myself anything, I've proven so time and time again.
